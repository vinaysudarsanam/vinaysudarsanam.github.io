My Goals.
I aim to collaborate with public health practitioners, clinicians, and government officials to develop interventions that improve access to and quality of healthcare globally. I hope to identify strategies to reduce cases of acute conditions and improve quality of care for chronic conditions while taking into account local cultures and health systems. By improving communication between patients, providers, and policymakers and reducing inequities in healthcare access and quality, I hope to improve the quality of life of individuals worldwide.
