Worldwide Healing Hands

Field Researcher, Public Health.

With the organization Worldwide Healing Hands, I am visiting Kampala, Uganda in the Summer of 2024. By collaborating with district health officers and public health professionals, I hope to assess education and healthcare access levels for the local Batwa community and develop a report summarizing the issues that must be solved to ensure equitable access moving forward.
